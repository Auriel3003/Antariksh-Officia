We have been conducting research on the topic. This research delves into wireless communication, specifically multiple array antennas communication in diverse mediums.

Automated Optical Telescopes

As part of a curricular project, Club members developed an Automated Optical Telescope, capable of tracking celestial bodies autonomously, eliminating the need for manual intervention. This Telescope is to be set on the Lawn of VIT for Solar and Lunar Observations, nearly soon.

Construction and Analysis using Horn Antenna

The CLub had the privilege of volunteering for the Horn Antenna Workshop, where we supported Jameer Manur Sir in the field of Instrumentation and Electronics for Horn Antennas. We also had the opportunity to attend lectures by distinguished experts, such as Dhruba Saikia Sir (Head, IUCAA) and Prof. Avinash Deshpande Sir (Hon. Prof, IIT Kanpur).
